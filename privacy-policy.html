Privacy Policy

At Shop AI, your privacy is our priority. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our app. By accessing or using Shop AI, you consent to the practices described below.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide and improve our services:

Personal Information

  • Name/Nickname: If you provide a name or nickname during onboarding, it is stored locally on your device for personalization. This information is not transmitted to our servers.
  • Contact Data: If you contact us for support or feedback (e.g., via email), we may collect your name, email address, and any information you choose to share in that communication.
  • Payment Details: Payment information is handled securely by the Apple App Store and our payment provider. We do not store financial details on our servers.

Usage Data

  • App interaction details like features accessed, session duration, and navigation flows.
  • Device information: model, OS version, app version, anonymous device ID.
  • Log data: IP address, timestamps for security and analytics.

User Input (AI Queries)

The content you enter in the AI features is sent to OpenAI’s ChatGPT for generating responses. We don’t store this data, but OpenAI may process it temporarily.

Note: Shop AI does not require account registration or profile creation. All data is stored locally or processed by third-party services.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To Provide and Personalize Services
  • AI Functionality: Input data is used to generate responses via ChatGPT.
  • App Improvement: Usage data helps us optimize the app.
  • Communication: We may contact you if you reached out to us.
  • Payments and Subscription Management
  • Legal Compliance and Enforcement

3. Data Sharing

3.1 Service Providers

  • Apple App Store: Handles payments and subscriptions.
  • Superwall: Manages subscriptions and paywall flows.
  • OpenAI: Processes your AI queries.
  • Analytics/Crash Reporting: (If used) for app stability and performance.

3.2 Legal Compliance

We may disclose your data when required by law or to protect rights and safety.

3.3 Business Transfers

Your data may be transferred in case of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale.

4. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures including encryption and secure local storage. However, no system is 100% secure.

5. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access and portability
  • Correction
  • Deletion
  • Restriction of processing
  • Withdrawal of consent

To exercise these rights, contact us at info@laionex.com.

6. Data Retention

  • On-Device Data: Deleted when you remove the app.
  • AI Query Data: Temporarily handled by OpenAI.
  • Subscription Data: Maintained for billing/legal reasons.
  • Support Communications: Kept only as long as necessary.

7. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We don’t use traditional cookies, but similar tracking tools like:
